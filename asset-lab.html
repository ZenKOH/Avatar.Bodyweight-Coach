Avatar Bodyweight Coach · 3D Asset Lab

Open model, rigging and motion pipeline

This page converts the app from a browser-rendered avatar prototype into a practical path toward real 3D avatars. It records free and open-source model routes, motion-capture sources, licence cautions, and a local manifest workflow. It does not bundle third-party human models until redistribution rights are clear.

Recommended pipeline

1. Human modelMakeHuman, VALID, Anny, or carefully reviewed MB-Lab output.
2. Rig and cleanBlender skeleton, clothing checks, joint limits, safe adult avatar.
3. Motion sourceExercise mocap, BVH, FBX, GLB animation clips or reviewed keyframes.
4. ExportGLB preferred for browser delivery; keep named animation clips.
5. IntegrateLoad through Three.js, map clips to exercise cards and voice cues.
