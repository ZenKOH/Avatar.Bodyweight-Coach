Safety scope

This app is educational. It does not diagnose, prescribe, assess form, or replace a clinician, coach, therapist, teacher, parent, or guardian. Stop if you feel pain, dizziness, chest discomfort, faintness, or unusual shortness of breath. Choose easier options when unsure.
